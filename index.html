The Studio of Simulation and Pataphysics is an interdisciplinary art and design studio investigating how models, media, and computational systems shape the ways we perceive, imagine, and make sense of the world. The studio collaborates with academic research groups, cultural institutions, public organisations, and industry partners to create research-driven design projects, installations, and experimental prototypes.
